Staff Picks

Still can't seem to find a flick, don't sweat it ! Go with one of our staff picks instead.

In the mood for something scary ?

Scream (1996)

"I'm usually too much a of a chicken to watch these kinds of movies, but in my opinion this movie isn't as scary as it is suspenseful. Slasher films are notorious for having bad acting, but Neve Campbell and Courtney Cox give stellar performances. Sure, it has its corny moments, but its clever writing and 90's charm make it a great choice for any movie night."
-Spike Spiegel

In the mood for something funny ?

Shrek 2 (2004)

"It's not often that a sequel comes along and outdoes the original, but Shrek 2 is the exception. The movie is heartfelt, funny, and not to mention beautifully animated. It's definitely a must watch!"
-Jet Black

In the mood for romantic ?

My Best Friend's Wedding (1997)

"This movie might look like another predictable "chick-flick," but it's actually far from it. It has comedy, drama, romance, and Julia Roberts. This movie has it all!"
-Faye Valentine
